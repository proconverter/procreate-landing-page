Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to install any software?

No, not at all. Our converter is a web-based tool that runs directly in your browser on any device, including your iPad, iPhone, Mac, or PC. No downloads, no installations.

Are the converted PNGs transparent?

No. To ensure maximum compatibility and processing speed for all users, our tool extracts the stamp images with a solid white background. This guarantees a fast and reliable conversion every time.

Is my data secure? Are my brushsets stored?

Your privacy is our top priority. We never store your brushsets. Your files are processed in memory and are automatically deleted from our servers within one hour of your session ending. We have no access to your original files.

What if a brushset contains more than just stamps?

Our tool is specifically designed to find and extract high-resolution stamp images (1024px or larger ) from your `.brushset` files. It intelligently ignores complex painting brushes or textures that don't have a simple stamp shape, ensuring you only get the usable assets.
