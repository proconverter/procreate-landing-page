Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to install any software? +

Not at all! Our converter is a web-based tool that works directly in your browser on any device, including desktops, iPads, and iPhones. All you need is your license key and your `.brushset` files.

How do I find my `.brushset` files on my iPad? +

That's a great question! On an iPad, you first need to export your brushes from Procreate to the Files app. It's a quick two-step process: 1. In Procreate: Open the Brush Library, swipe left on the brush set you want to convert, and tap "Share". 2. Save to Files: In the share menu that pops up, choose "Save to Files". We recommend creating a new folder named "Brush Exports" to keep things organized. Once you've done that, you can easily find and select the `.brushset` file from that folder when you use our converter tool.

Are the converted PNG files transparent? +

To ensure maximum compatibility and processing speed, our tool currently extracts stamps with a solid white background. This guarantees a fast and reliable conversion every time.

Is it safe? Do you keep my brushes? +

Your privacy is our top priority. We never store your `.brushset` files. They are processed on our secure servers and are automatically deleted within one hour of conversion.
